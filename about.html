About Foodee

Foodee is your go-to campus food ordering system designed to simplify your dining experience. Whether you're a student craving quick snacks or a staff member managing canteen orders, Foodee connects everyone through a fast, intuitive platform.

With real-time menus, seamless order placement, and easy order tracking, Foodee enhances your food journey on campus. Our goal is to provide a delightful and convenient solution to everyday food needs within the college environment.
